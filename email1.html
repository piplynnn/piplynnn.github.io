The All Purpose Car Dashboard is a real-time Unity application that connects directly to your vehicle via OBD-II. Whether you're tracking a 0–60 run, watching your RPM ladder climb, or monitoring gear shifts under load — this dashboard gives you a cockpit-grade view of every data point your car produces.

Live OBD-II Data Stream

Reads real-time engine data directly from your vehicle's ECU — RPM, speed, throttle, and more — rendered the moment your engine breathes.

RPM Ladder & Shift Lights

A segmented RPM ladder lights up as revs climb, with programmable shift-light triggers built for performance driving awareness.

0–60 MPH Timer

Automatic launch detection and millisecond-accurate 0–60 tracking, so you always know where your car's performance stands.

Custom UI Built in Unity

Designed and coded from scratch using Unity UI Toolkit — clean, responsive, and tuned for high-contrast readability at a glance.
