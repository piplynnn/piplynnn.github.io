Your car's data,

The All Purpose Car Dashboard is a real-time Unity application that plugs directly into your vehicle via OBD-II — giving you cockpit-grade visibility into every metric your engine produces.

Unity OBD-II C# UI Toolkit

Built for drivers who want more than a stock gauge cluster.

Whether you're tracking a 0–60 run, watching your RPM ladder light up, or monitoring gear changes under load — the dashboard surfaces the data that actually matters, in real time.

Connect any OBD-II Bluetooth or USB adapter, launch the app, and your engine's data starts flowing immediately. No configuration headaches. No third-party subscriptions.

The UI was designed from scratch in Unity UI Toolkit — high contrast, minimal clutter, and readable at a glance even while driving.

Live OBD-II Stream
