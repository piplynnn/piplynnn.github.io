Real-time OBD-II telemetry. Animated RPM ladder. Precision 0–60 timing. This week I'm sharing the full demo walkthrough of the All Purpose Car Dashboard — and a closer look at how it was built.

demo.mp4 — v1 Live data

See every rev, every shift, every run.

The demo above shows the dashboard running live with real OBD-II input — the RPM ladder climbing in real time, shift lights triggering at the programmed threshold, and the 0–60 timer capturing a full acceleration run.

Everything you see is driven by actual engine data parsed through the OBD-II serial connection and rendered at 60fps in Unity.

The UI adapts dynamically — redline zones shift based on the current gear, and the layout is designed to keep your eyes forward, not buried in numbers.

"Built it because I couldn't find a dashboard app that actually showed what I wanted to see at a glance."

— Piplynn Motorsports
